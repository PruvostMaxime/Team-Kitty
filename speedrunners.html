SPEEDRUNNERS

In a city filled with superheroes, getting to crimes becomes a competition on its own. Fortunately there are enough rockets, bombs, grappling hooks, spikes, and other goodies lying around -- to make the competition fun and fast. Welcome to SpeedRunners. SpeedRunners is a 4 player competitive platformer with grappling hooks, power-ups, and interactive environments. Run, jump, swing around, fire rockets, grapple onto people to knock them off screen.

Key Features

  1. 4 Player Competitive Platforming!
  2. Keep up with the fastest player in-game, or fall offscreen and get eliminated!
  3. Use weapons like missiles, mines and grappling hooks to screw over your friends, and make new enemies!
  4. Local and online multiplayer! Can you have 2 players on a couch against 2 other players on the internet? Yes of course!
  5. Bots enabled - play on your own with several AI opponents. Or put them into matches with other people!
  6. A tutorial teaches you how to play
  7. Controller compatible (and recommended!)
  8. A dozen expertly designed maps
  9. Unique game modifiers
  10. A character named Unic!
  11. Full blown level editor with over 10k user created levels
  12. An exciting single player campaign
  13. Create your own custom in-game trails (visible when you hit super-speed)
